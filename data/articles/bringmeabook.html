Overview

    Challenge

  • The Bring Me a Book Foundation came to MK Partners realizing it was not utilizing salesforce.com at its full capacity. As a result, it could not effectively track donors or the efficacy of the programs they run.

    Solutions

  • MK Partners streamlined Bring Me a Book Foundation's instance of salesforce.com to reflect its business needs.
  • We optimized donor management process to track targeted campaigns and appeals, track the efficacy of fundraising events and attendance and e-appeals with salesforce.com. Thank you notes to donors are automatically processed to bolster donor relationships.
  • MK Partners developed an automated program management process to track the efficacy of the First Teachers and Bookcase Library (BCL) programs. This solution enabled Bring Me a Book Foundation to contact the teachers they work with at the push of a button.
